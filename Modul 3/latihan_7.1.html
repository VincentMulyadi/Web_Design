Adventurer Guide

  • How To be a good adventurer :
    1. Pick a suitable job/class
    2. Prioritize your/teammate safety above all else
    3. Prepare you gear and potions throughly
    4. Gather all the information necessary before diving thorugh dungeons
    5. Coordinate with your teammates(Teamwork is Key)

  • Main Adventurer Classes/Jobs:
    1. Warrior
    2. Archer
    3. Mage
    4. Healer/Support
    5. Tamer
